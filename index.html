SPORTS CAR DEFINITION

According To Wikipedia:
A sports car is a car designed with an emphasis on dynamic performance, such as handling, acceleration, top speed, or thrill of driving.
Some people mistakenly consider all road going “sports cars” to be the same. When in fact there are four distinct categories:

  1. Sports Car
  2. High-Performance Sports Car
  3. Super car
  4. Hyper car
